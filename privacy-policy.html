Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Last Updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

At Mobile Viewer Io, we strive to collect the minimum amount of data possible to provide our services:

  • Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous information about how visitors use our site (e.g., page views, time on site).
  • Account Information: If you sign up for a Pro account, we collect your email address and billing information through our secure payment processor.
  • Contact Data: Information provided via our contact form is used only to respond to your requests.

2. What We DO NOT Collect

We believe your work is your business. We do not track, store, or log the URLs you enter into our mobile viewer emulator. Your development links and project previews remain private to your local session.

3. Use of Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences (such as your preferred device zoom or theme) and to analyze our traffic. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features of the tool may not function correctly.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services for specific functions:

  • Google Analytics: For website traffic analysis.
  • Stripe: For secure payment processing. We never store your credit card details on our servers.
  • Google AdSense: To show relevant advertisements to help keep our basic tools free.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location (such as the EU under GDPR), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. Please contact us if you wish to exercise these rights.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
