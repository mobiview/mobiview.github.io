Seamless Mobile Onboarding

In 2026, the QR code has evolved from a simple marketing gimmick into a critical tool for **User Experience (UX)**. Whether you are testing a Progressive Web App (PWA) on a physical device or inviting users to a beta testing group, a high-resolution QR code ensures that the handoff from desktop to mobile is frictionless.

How to Generate Your QR Code

  1. Enter Content: Paste your website URL, deep link, or plain text into the input field.
  2. Choose Resolution: Select a size. Use larger dimensions (1024px) for print materials and smaller ones for digital screens.
  3. Test and Download: Before downloading, use your mobile camera to verify the scan. Once confirmed, save the PNG for your documentation or marketing assets.
