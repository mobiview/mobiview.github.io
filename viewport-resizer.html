Precision Testing for Modern Breakpoints

The **Viewport Resizer** is an essential tool for developers who need to move beyond standard device presets. While many tools offer "Mobile" or "Tablet" views, modern CSS requires testing for the "in-between" states—the exact pixels where your media queries trigger.

Mastering the Fluid Web

By manually adjusting the width and height, you can find the exact breaking point of your navigation menus, flexbox layouts, and typography. This tool simulates the viewport environment, allowing you to ensure that content never overflows and accessibility remains intact throughout the resize transition.

"A truly responsive site isn't just one that looks good at 320px and 1200px; it's one that remains functional at every single pixel in between."
