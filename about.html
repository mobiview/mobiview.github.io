Our Mission

The Story of Mobile Viewer

Started in 2024, Mobile Viewer Io was born out of a simple frustration: testing responsive websites shouldn't require twenty physical devices or expensive, bloated software. We wanted a tool that was fast, browser-based, and beautiful.

What began as a simple viewport resizer has grown into a comprehensive Professional Developer Toolkit. We believe that high-quality testing tools should be accessible to everyone, from solo freelancers to large-scale enterprise teams.
